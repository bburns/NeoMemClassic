Views

Overview

There are several different views available in NeoMem - the views on the left side of the screen let you navigate among all the objects in the file, while the views on the right side show information about the currently selected item. Different views may be available on the right side depending on the class of the selected object.

Each object has a Text View associated with it, where you can enter text with a simple word processor. Each object may also have a Contents View which shows the contents of the selected object, and a Properties View which shows the properties associated with the selected object.

You can switch among the views by clicking on the tab containing its name, by pressing Alt+PageDown or Alt+PageUp to cycle through the visible views, or by pressing the Alt key plus the letter underlined in the tab.
